## Particularities of Emerald RNG Manipulation
 - Check [Particularities of GBA RNG Manipulation](/gba-overview)
 - Pokémon Emerald is the easiest GBA game for RNG manipulation because its initial seed is always 0.

## Points of Interest
- Finding your [SID](/gen3-sid), which is required to RNG manipulate a shiny Pokémon.

- RNG manipulating Pokémon to control Shininess, IVs, and Nature.
  - Wild Pokémon (Sweet Scent, Fishing, Rock Smash)
  - Stationary Pokémon
  - Egg Pokémon

- RNG manipulation to get access to Mirage Island, which is the only way to get Liechi Berry

- RNG manipulation the Battle Frontier to ensure easy victories

- RNG manipulation to get Pokérus, which is notably required to get optimal low-level Pokémon such as Level 5 Smeargle with Dragon Rage
